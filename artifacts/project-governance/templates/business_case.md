# Business Case / Value Case

## Purpose

Capture why the initiative should exist, what value it creates, and what cost or risk it introduces.

## Fields

- initiative name
- problem or opportunity
- expected benefits
- delivery cost
- key risks
- success criteria
- decision status

## Usage

1. Keep the statement concise enough for executive review.
2. Tie every claimed benefit to a measurable outcome.
3. Record the assumptions that materially affect the value case.
