## Policy Statement

Document only what improves understanding, reuse, traceability, or operational safety, and keep the document type aligned with the reader need.

## Requirements

- Distinguish instance documents from reusable templates.
- Keep provenance and attribution explicit.
- Publish only the level of detail appropriate for the artifact classification.
- Use frontmatter and source attribution for Markdown documents that are reusable.
