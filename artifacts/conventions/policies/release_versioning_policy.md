## Policy Statement

Define release naming, versioning, communication, and rollback expectations for repositories that publish operational or user-facing changes.

## Requirements

- Declare versioning rules.
- Declare release approval and publication rules.
- Declare how release notes are produced and reviewed.
- Declare how rollback readiness is proven.
