## Policy Statement

Treat knowledge as a managed asset with creation, review, update, archive, and retirement stages.

## Requirements

- Every knowledge artifact has an owner and review cadence.
- Stale or superseded material is archived with traceability.
- Knowledge artifacts preserve provenance and applicability.
- Automation should flag lifecycle events where possible.
