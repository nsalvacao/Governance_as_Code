## Policy Statement

Record architecture-shaping decisions when the cost of re-discovery exceeds the cost of writing the decision down.

## Requirements

- Use ADRs for hard-to-reverse or high-impact decisions.
- Link the decision log entry to the ADR where appropriate.
- Keep rationale concise and scoped to the decision.
- Preserve traceability to the source basis and review cadence.
