## Purpose

Track risks in a structured way so owners can assess, mitigate, and review them consistently.

## Required fields

- Risk ID
- Description
- Likelihood
- Impact
- Owner
- Mitigation
- Status
- Review date

## Guidance

- Keep one risk per row.
- Separate current state from intended mitigation.
- Make the review cadence explicit.
