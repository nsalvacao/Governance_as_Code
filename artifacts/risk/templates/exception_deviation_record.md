## Purpose

Record deliberate deviations from policy or standard so that exceptions stay visible and time-bounded.

## Required fields

- Exception ID
- Policy or control affected
- Reason
- Risk accepted
- Approver
- Expiry or review date
- Compensating controls

## Guidance

- Make the exception temporary unless explicitly renewed.
- State the approval boundary clearly.
- Link the exception to the risk register where appropriate.
