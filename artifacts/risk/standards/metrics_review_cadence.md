## Purpose

Define how often risks, exceptions, and governance metrics should be reviewed so the system stays current.

## Rules

- Review cadence must be explicit.
- Metrics must be tied to a decision or action.
- Reviews should produce either confirmation, mitigation, or escalation.

## Guidance

- Choose cadences that fit the volatility of the record.
- Keep review owners obvious.
- Use the cadence to trigger automation where possible.
