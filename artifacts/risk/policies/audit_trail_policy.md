## Policy Statement

Material decisions, changes, exceptions, and risk actions must remain traceable enough for review and reconstruction.

## Requirements

- Retain the identifier, owner, and date of each material record.
- Keep references to source evidence and linked artifacts.
- Preserve enough context to explain why a record exists and who approved it.

## Guidance

- Favor explicit links over hidden context.
- Keep the trail readable for humans and automatable for agents.
