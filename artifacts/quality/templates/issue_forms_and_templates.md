## Purpose

Provide a reusable intake structure for issue reporting so requests arrive with the minimum information needed to act.

## Template family

- **Bug report:** `{{BUG_REPORT_FIELDS}}`
- **Change request:** `{{CHANGE_REQUEST_FIELDS}}`
- **Incident report:** `{{INCIDENT_REPORT_FIELDS}}`
- **Investigation request:** `{{INVESTIGATION_REQUEST_FIELDS}}`
- **Configuration:** `{{ISSUE_TEMPLATE_CONFIG}}`

## Guidance

- Keep prompts short and unambiguous.
- Ask only for fields that materially improve triage or routing.
- Align each form with the downstream workflow that consumes it.
