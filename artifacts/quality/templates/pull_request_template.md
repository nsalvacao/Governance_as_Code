## Purpose

Standardize the information a contributor provides when proposing a change.

## Template

- **Summary:** `{{SUMMARY}}`

- **Automation and AI notes:** `{{AUTOMATION_NOTES}}`

## Guidance

- Keep the prompt concise and reusable.
- Ask contributors to point to validation evidence, not just intent.
- Preserve placeholder-based parameterization for downstream adoption.
