## Policy Statement

Define a deterministic incident-management model that makes roles, severity, escalation, and recovery decisions explicit.

## Requirements

- Assign incident commander, communications lead, and technical owner roles.
- Define severity and response expectations.
- Require incident reports and post-incident learning artifacts.
- Link to playbooks, runbooks, and continuity plans.
