## Purpose

Record reusable lessons that should change future behavior, not just summarize a past event.

## Required sections

- Context
- Lesson
- Why it matters
- Recommended change
- Owner
- Review trigger

## Guidance

- Phrase each lesson so it can be applied in a later repository or service.
- Keep the lesson separate from the corrective action list.
- Use evidence pointers when the lesson comes from a concrete event.
