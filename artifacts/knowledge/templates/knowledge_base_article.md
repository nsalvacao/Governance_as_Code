## Purpose

Capture a stable answer, procedure, or reference that can be reused by humans and agents.

## Required sections

- Summary

- Prerequisites
- Procedure or reference content
- Validation
- Related documents

## Guidance

- Keep the article narrowly scoped.
- State the audience and applicability explicitly.
- Link to higher-order standards or policies when the article is an instantiation of them.
