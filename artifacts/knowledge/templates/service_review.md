## Purpose

Review service health and operational trends so that reliability work stays visible and actionable.

## Required sections

- Service scope
- Health signals
- SLO status
- Incidents and trends
- Improvements in progress
- Decisions and follow-up

## Guidance

- Use quantitative evidence wherever possible.
- Keep the review focused on changes since the last review.
- Tie each decision to an owner and a next checkpoint.
