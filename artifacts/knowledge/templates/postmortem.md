## Purpose

Capture a blameless operational review that explains what happened, what it affected, and what we will change.

## Required sections

- Summary
- Customer and operational impact
- Timeline
- Detection and response
- Contributing factors
- Corrective actions
- What worked well
- Follow-up ownership

## Guidance

- Keep the narrative concise and evidence-backed.
- Distinguish confirmed facts from hypotheses.
- Link to logs, dashboards, and incident records where available.
- Use placeholders for service-specific details so the template can be instantiated safely.
