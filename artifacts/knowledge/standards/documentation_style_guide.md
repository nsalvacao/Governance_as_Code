## Purpose

Define the editorial rules that keep documentation consistent, clear, and automation-friendly.

## Rules

- Use concise, direct language.
- Prefer section headings that describe the reader goal.
- Keep procedures action-oriented and numbered.
- Keep cross-links explicit.
- Use placeholders consistently for downstream instantiation.

## Guidance

- Optimize for scanning and reuse, not prose polish.
- Preserve attribution and source traceability in every public artifact.
- Treat examples as examples, not hidden policy.
