## Policy Statement

Documents that are no longer authoritative must be deprecated, marked clearly, and archived according to a predictable lifecycle.

## Requirements

- State when a document is superseded.
- Preserve redirect or replacement links where useful.
- Keep archive decisions traceable in the decision log.
- Distinguish active, deprecated, and archived states in frontmatter.

## Guidance

- Do not silently remove important historical material.
- Keep retired documents discoverable but clearly non-authoritative.
