## Policy Statement

Adopt a clear code of conduct that defines expected behavior, unacceptable behavior, and the moderation path for violations.

## Policy Requirements

- State expected behavior and respectful collaboration norms.
- State what is not acceptable.
- Define reporting and escalation channels.
- Define who can enforce the policy and how decisions are communicated.
